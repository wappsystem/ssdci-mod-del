The Buschke Selective Reminding Test (SRT)


Instructions for administration

"This test is to see how quickly you can learn a list of words. I'm going to read you a list of 12 word. I want you to listen carefully because when I stop, I want you to to tell me as many of the words you can remember. The words do not have to be in any particular order. When you have given me all the words that you can remember, I will tell you the words that you didn't give m from the list; thenI want you to tell me the entire list all over again."

Read at rate of 1 word per 2 secs.

Trial2+:"There are the words you missed. Now try to tell me the whole list of 12 words even if you told them to me before."
